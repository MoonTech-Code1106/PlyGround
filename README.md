# How to Use??
To *USE/PLAY* the engine here is directions:
1. Press **1** to get a Ball
2. Press **2** to get a *Textured* Box
3. Press **3** to get a Triangle
4. Press **4** to get a Rod
5. Press **5** to get a Pentagon
6. Press **6** to get a Hexagon
7. Press **7** to get a Plank
8. Press **8** to get a Small Ball
-------------------------
Press **T** to get a Torch to light Box and Plank
Press **X** to get a **BoomBox(TNT)** and E to Ignite it, ****DANGER!!!****, Igntines nearby object and pushes them
